About us







We develop website named HARP, stands for Highly Automated enteRtainment Platform that acts as a bridge between customer and performers. HARP allows people to make requests for performers. It can be for private needs such as wedding ceremony and feasts all the way to bussiness performance like during a company’s annual dinner. Performers can register themselves on HARP and will be advertised once we give the approval. HARP will annihilate the trouble for anybody that seeks satisfactory level of performance but does not have the time to make an appointment or does not know where to start. It also allows those who want to perform but have yet to find a suitable platform for them to begin. HARP was created using HTML markup language and CSC equivalent technology. The main features of HARP are well organized directory menu, eye-pleasant home page and online requests with payment made through trusted online accounts such as local banks and Paypal. Visitors that has not yet confirmed about making a request for a performance can just surf the site and plan for themselves. Visitors can browse through them, picks whoever in favors and available, fill in the required information, click on Confirm Request which will redirect them to a chosen secured payment site and done! A wonderful and unforgettable performance will be right at your doorstep. For performers, they just have to register themselves online through our HARP. They will have to go through some interviews and if approved, they can start performing right away if someone made a request. All in all, HARP creates an environment where anybody can have a spectacular performance for any kind of ocassion with less trouble and more succession, performers can make some money, everything online in just minutes.
